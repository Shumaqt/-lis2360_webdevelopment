Cool Sites To Learn More About Making Songs:

MySongCoach MySongCoach is a website designed to teach upcoming and future artists the art of creating songs from scratch. It covers the essential topics of lyrics, beatmaking, and looping in an easy-to-follow set of guidelines. https://mysongcoach.com/
Soundcloud Soundcloud is a free music streaming platform that allows users to discover the various genres of music. It can be used by artists to stream their individual work and provide them with endless sources of songs from a specific genre. https://soundcloud.com/
Coursera Coursera provides an in-depth six week course that dives into the critical components of songmaking. It is entirely online and utilizes the structure of academic course in order to push the user to consistently practice the art. https://www.coursera.org/learn/songwriting-lyrics

MasterWriter

 In MasterWriter, artists that are just beginning to write music have multiple options in making the songwriting process more simple and efficient. They offer a free-trial which allows people to use the program before committing to the monthly subscription. Even if you are an experienced songwriter, this system is designed to take your skills to the next level. https://masterwriter.com/songwriters/
Tunedly What is point of creating songs without recording them? Tunedly gives you the opportunity to record your music on an online platform for your convenience. After the final product is mastered, artists also have multiple options for which streaming platforms they want their music available on. https://www.tunedly.com/
