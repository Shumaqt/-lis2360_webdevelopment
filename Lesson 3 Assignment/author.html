About the Author

Shumaq Tahir

Shumaq Tahir is a student at the Florida State University. He is majoring in ICT and plans to pursue music development after college. Music has been a large part of his life since he was a child. He grew up in a very musical hometown and his inspirations are Eric Clapton, J.Cole, and Bruno Mars.
