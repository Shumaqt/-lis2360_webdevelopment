Don't be normal. Be you.

Don't be the cool kid and throw trash out of your window while you're driving. That is not cool. Trust me, your friends will respect you much more if you show respect to the earth.

Your body is an ecosystem. Take care of the earth as if you're taking care of yourself.

If a car sits out in sun without being used, over time it will get old and rusty. This same mechanism works for humans and the earth. Lifting weights and exercising makes you stronger. Recycling makes the earth stronger.

Have you ever been blown away by an image of the ocean or land? It's a pretty cool sight. If you enjoy watching shows such as National Geographic, go recycle something today in hopes for more episodes and a better world.
